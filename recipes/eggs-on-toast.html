Eggs on toast

Description

There is nothing more iconic (and simple!) to make for breakfast than eggs on toast. This nutrient dense meal can be made in less than 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp salted butter
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 slice of bread
  • Ground pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Ground turmeric

Steps

  1. Put a small, nonstick pan on medium heat
  2. Melt the butter and a small drizzle of olive oil together in the pan
  3. Crack the eggs gently into the pan, taking care to not break the yolks
  4. Add your sprinkling of pepper, garlic, and turmeric to your taste
  5. Once the bottom of the eggs whites have completely set (opaque and not see through), gently flip the eggs with a spatula, taking care again to not break the yolks
  6. Allow the yolk side to cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute, to desired yolk consistency(I prefer mine slightly set on the outside but runny on the inside)
  7. Remove the cooked eggs from the pan an onto a plate to rest
  8. Add your bread to the still hot pan, allowing it to soak up the remaining oil and butter
  9. Once the now buttered bread is crispy and toasted on both sides, plate your eggs on to the toast and enjoy!
